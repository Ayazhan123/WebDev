Recipe #1

  • Name: Tiramisu

savoyage cookies 300g
condensed milk 120g

Step 2: Mix with condensed milk

Recipe #2

  • Name: Honey cake
  • Time: 60min
Ingredients Quantity
flour 450g
honey 80g
eggs 3
butter 200g

Step 1: Mix all the ingredients and knead the dough
Step 2: Bake each cake separately in the oven at 180 degrees
Step 3: Assemble the cake alternating cake and cream
